Hellow ! I'm currently a Research Assistant and at my last semester of M.Sc. in Climate Modelling and Risk Management at Institute of Flood and Water Management, BUET, Bangladesh. 

I'm working on the <b>Web-Ais</b> Project currently where I'm developing a Irrigiation Water Requirment and Scheduling model. Most of project work requires remote sensing and advanced data wrangling. Among my courseworks, I love working with Delft3D and HEC-RAS, trying to model the erosion of Halda River. In future, I hope to work in the relatively unknown and turbulent field of 'Turbulence' (pun intended 😁).

In my free time, I love long walks, studying History and watching movies.
