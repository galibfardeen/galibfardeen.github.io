My research extends to the behaviour of reinforced concrete members and connections under various loading conditions, including CFRP-strengthened systems and impact scenarios.

### Ongoing Work

**CFRP Strengthened Beam-Column Connections**
- Investigating the response of CFRP-strengthened RC beam-column connections under both **monotonic and cyclic loading**.
- Developing numerical models validated against experimental results.
- *Manuscript in submission — Journal of Steel Construction Research (2025)*

**Impact Behaviour of Pressurised uPVC Pipes**
- Experimental and numerical analysis of uPVC pipes under impact loading.
- Combines laboratory testing with finite element simulation.
- *Manuscript in preparation (2025)*

### Methods

- Finite element modelling in ABAQUS with Concrete Damaged Plasticity (CDP) model
- Embedded reinforcement modelling with bond and shrinkage properties
- Cyclic loading protocols and monotonic pushover analysis
